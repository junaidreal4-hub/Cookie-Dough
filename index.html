Our Story

Made with Heart

Cookie and Dough was born out of a simple belief — that the best things in life are homemade. Nestled in the beautiful Andaman Islands, we bake every item fresh, by hand, with no shortcuts and no compromises.


From our signature stuffed cookies to our indulgent brownies and traditional Indian desserts, every recipe is crafted with care and served with love. We are not just a bakery — we are a homemade experience.
